Bob's Smile Makeover - No More Turkey Teeth!

Looking for a trusted dentist near me or a reliable dental clinic near me can often feel overwhelming, especially when considering complex restorative treatments abroad. Bob’s story is a great example of how patients visiting Emerald Dental Clinic in Tirana can receive honest guidance, modern treatment planning, and high-quality dental care without unnecessary procedures.

A few weeks before visiting Albania, Bob initially contacted our clinic by email simply to request more information regarding his dental condition and possible treatment options. After arriving in Tirana, he decided to walk into our clinic one morning for a consultation. Fortunately, our schedule was free at that moment, allowing him to immediately benefit from an extensive hour-long consultation with both our oral surgeon and one of our dentists.

During the consultation, Bob had many questions regarding his dental health, previous treatments, long-term durability, and the best restorative approach for his case. Our team carefully explained every stage of the process in detail, helping him fully understand the condition of his teeth and the available treatment options. He was extremely satisfied with the transparency and professionalism of the consultation.

Bob had undergone several root canal treatments a few years earlier. After performing a full evaluation and confirming his oral health condition with a 2D panoramic X-ray, our dental team determined that the best course of action was to preserve his healthy natural teeth whenever possible. Instead of recommending unnecessary extractions and dental implants, our dentists chose a more conservative and biologically favorable solution by restoring the teeth with zirconia crowns and zirconia dental bridges.

The treatment process began with teeth prepping, followed by taking an impressions. Using advanced CAD/CAM digital dentistry technology, the zirconia bridges were carefully designed and fabricated in our in-house dental laboratory. This workflow allowed our team to maintain excellent precision, aesthetics, and structural durability throughout the restoration process.

In the video above, you can see the cementing and final placement of the zirconia bridges, as well as the impressive before-and-after transformation of Bob’s smile, creating the final result shown in the footage.

Bob was extremely happy with both the appearance and functionality of his new smile, and we have to say that our dental team did an outstanding job.
