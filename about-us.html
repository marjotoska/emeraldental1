About Us

Why choose us?

We only use high quality manufactured dental products in Switzerland and Germany for dental implants, crowns and aesthetic veneers. We provide warranty and passport of certification for implants. We are specialized in the multidisciplinary approach for complex situations.

Emerald Dental is one of the most prestigious dental clinics in Southeastern Europe, because:

The dental equipment were renovated on July of 2019, from well-known, reputable Italian and French manufacturers such as OMS, Newmed & Acteon.

Our quality of the dental works meets EU standards (EN 1641:2009 - Dentistry), while keeping the prices 70% cheaper compared to your country!

Top Location and Services

Emerald Dental is a modern dental clinic conveniently located within 5 minutes from the city center of the capital Tirana. We are specialized in advanced diagnostics and treatment of dental and oral disorders. In addition to high-end dental equipment, all services are provided in a comfortable, luxury environment. New patients are welcomed with a complimentary oral health consultation.

Professional and Competent Doctors

Our doctors have studied on the best universities that Italy, Germany and France has to offer, with 75% of them possessing a doctorate degree. They have 12-20 years of working experience with patients, especially on Dental Implants and Veneers.

What sets us apart from others?

Our clinic possesses certifications that comply to EU Standards. The comfortable environment, warm staff and even the decorative wall-mounted aquarium will alleviate your dental anxiety/phobia and make you feel like home. We use the best treatment techniques and equipment to make your dental tourism experience perfect!
