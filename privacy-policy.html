Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Emerald Dental Clinic collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is used solely for your dental care and treatment planning.

Key Principles

We collect and process personal data only to:

  • Understand your health condition
  • Offer accurate diagnoses and personalized treatment plans
  • Contact you regarding your dental appointments or inquiries

We never sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Your data is handled with strict confidentiality and used only by authorized personnel at Emerald Dental Clinic.

Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information that identifies you, including your name, email, phone number, medical history, and appointment records.
  • Service: Refers to the dental services and website offered by Emerald Dental Clinic.
  • You: The individual using our Service (a patient, potential patient, or website visitor).

What Information We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address (if necessary for communication or billing)
  • Dental/Medical history provided through forms or consultations
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited on our website)

Use of Your Personal Data

We use your information strictly for purposes related to your dental health:

  • To assess your dental and general health
  • To recommend appropriate treatments
  • To schedule or confirm appointments
  • To communicate with you regarding our services
  • To improve the usability of our website

Confidentiality and Security

Your data is stored securely and accessed only by dental professionals and staff bound by confidentiality agreements. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies only to improve website functionality and understand how users interact with our site. No sensitive or medical information is stored in cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide you with dental care or as required by law (e.g., for health records). You can request deletion of your data unless we are legally obligated to keep it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally applicable)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Please email us to make such requests.

Third-Party Access

We do not share your personal data with any third-party companies, advertisers, or partners. The only exception is if legally required by authorities (e.g., a court order).

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents or guardians may contact us to remove data submitted by a child.

Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. If the changes are significant, you will be notified before they take effect.
