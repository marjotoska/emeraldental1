Preventive Dental Care and Oral Hygiene in Tirana

Maintaining proper oral hygiene is essential for healthy teeth and long-term wellness. At Emerald Dental Clinic, the premier dental clinic in Tirana, we provide modern preventive dental care and cosmetic treatments. Whether you need a routine dental cleaning or a complete smile makeover, our dentists in Albania offer world-class care at competitive prices.

Professional Teeth Cleaning and Deep Dental Cleaning

A professional dental cleaning is the foundation of oral health. Even with daily care, plaque can accumulate in hard-to-reach areas. Our dental hygiene treatment includes:

  • Professional plaque removal using ultrasonic instruments.
  • Deep dental cleaning and periodontal cleaning to treat the roots and gums.
  • Air polish dental techniques (including air powder polishing) to gently remove biofilm.
  • Polishing to smooth enamel and remove surface stains.

Regular visits every six months are the best way of preventing gum disease and ensuring effective bad breath treatment. By removing tartar, we provide a vital treatment for bleeding gums and prevent long-term tooth decay.

Professional Teeth Whitening (Dental Bleaching)

Professional teeth whitening is our most requested cosmetic service. Our experienced dentists use professional-grade systems to ensure safe, bright results.

Advantages of Professional Whitening:

  • Noticeable Results: Significantly lightens teeth compared to over-the-counter kits.
  • Safe for Enamel: Performed under expert supervision to ensure sensitive teeth relief.
  • Customized Care: We offer specific teeth whitening for sensitive teeth to minimize discomfort.

When Whitening May Not Be Recommended

While highly effective, whitening isn't for everyone. We provide specialized teeth whitening for fluorosis (white spots), though results vary. If you have internal trauma or existing crowns, we may suggest alternative professional stain removal or veneers.

Fluoride Treatment and Enamel Remineralization

To protect your smile against cavities, we offer advanced fluoride treatment. This dental fluoridation process is essential for enamel remineralization, making teeth more resistant to the acid attacks that cause decay.

  • Strengthens weakened enamel.
  • Provides significant sensitive teeth relief.
  • Protects patients undergoing orthodontic treatment.

How to Remove Tea and Coffee Stains from Teeth

If you are wondering how to remove tea and coffee stains from teeth, our Air Polish dental treatment is the answer. It is faster and more comfortable than traditional polishing and effectively erases lifestyle-related discoloration.

Deep Periodontal Cleaning (Professional Curettage)

When plaque and tartar accumulate below the gum line, a standard cleaning isn't enough. A periodontal cleaning also known as professional curettage or deep cleaning, is a specialized procedure designed to reach where toothbrushes cannot.

This periodontal deep cleaning is the gold standard for treating patients with gingivitis or early-stage periodontal disease, preventing bacteria from damaging the gums and the supporting bone structure.

What Happens During Periodontal Cleaning?

During your periodontal cleaning session, our specialists focus on four clinical goals to restore your oral health:

  • Subgingival Scaling: Removing stubborn tartar and bacterial deposits from below the gum line.
  • Pocket Debridement: Thoroughly cleaning the periodontal pockets surrounding each tooth.
  • Root Planing: Smoothing the root surfaces to encourage the gums to reattach securely.
  • Bacterial Control: Reducing inflammation and halting the growth of harmful pathogens.

Benefits of Periodontal Cleaning for Gum Health

As a highly effective non-surgical periodontal treatment, this procedure offers immediate and long-term advantages:

  • Stops the progression of active gum disease.
  • Provides a direct treatment for bleeding gums and chronic inflammation.
  • Eliminates deep-seated bacteria to ensure long-term oral stability.
  • Protects the jawbone and prevents tooth mobility and potential tooth loss.

Investing in a periodontal cleaning today is the most effective way to avoid more invasive surgical procedures in the future.
