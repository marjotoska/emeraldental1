Cost of Dental Implants and Veneers in Albania

We offer 70% lower prices compared to Italy.

Our dental materials come from reputable companies such as Implantswiss or Straumann. We provide a lifetime warranty for implants and over 20 years for dental crowns / veneers.

You can find a price list below. Please note that flight and accommodation costs are at our expense. Find out more about this by clicking on the link.

Dental Implant In Titanium, Abutment and Temporary Crown


On Emerald Dental the price of a single implant, together with abutment and crown, costs 500 euros. The dental implant has a lifetime guarantee. The procedure takes about 20 minutes and is not painful.

Price: €500

All on 4 Implants


The All-on-4 treatment is an effective solution that transforms your smile and restores the functionality of your teeth. Includes 4 dental implants, abutments and full denture for one arch. It is suitable if you have lost some of your teeth on your arch.

Price: €2900

All on 6 Implants


Compared to All On Four method, All On Six Implantology is preferable if almost all of the teeth have been lost in an arch, also being more stable and convenient.

Price: €3900

Metal-Ceramic Crown


Under ideal conditions of use, metal-ceramic crowns can last for a period of 15-20 years. However you should expect the crowns to be replaced after 7-10 years of use.

Price: €120

Zirconia Crown


One of the biggest advantages of a zirconia crown is its strength, durability and longevity, lasting about 15-20 years. It's also biocompatible, which means it won't cause allergic reactions to your body. Its color almost matches that of natural teeth, with the better choice being E-Max.

Price: €200

E-Max Faccette/Corone


E-Max are a special kind of porcelain veneers. E-max is more durable than others and can last 20 years or more with proper care. Its semi-transparent properties make it the best match to natural teeth.

Price: €300
